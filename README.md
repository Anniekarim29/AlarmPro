# AlarmPro

An alarm app that charges you money to stop ringing. Yes, you read that right. We took the concept of "pain makes you wake up" and added capitalism to it.

You set an alarm. It rings. You want it to stop. You pay. Simple.

We call it productivity. Your bank account calls it a nightmare.

---

## What It Does

- Shows a beautiful analog clock so you feel classy while losing money
- Rings an alarm at whatever cursed hour you set
- Makes you complete a payment before it shuts up
- Confirms with "Alarm silenced - Payment received" so you know exactly what just happened to your wallet
- Has an "ARM AGAIN" button for people who enjoy suffering
